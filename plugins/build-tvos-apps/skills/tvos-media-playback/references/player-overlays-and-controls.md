# Player Overlays And Controls

Player overlays exist to support transport and context, not to become a second application layered on top of video.

## Strong defaults

- Keep only the most important actions in the main overlay.
- Make every overlay element focus-safe and readable from a distance.
- Hide or dim nonessential chrome when the user is not actively controlling playback.
- Use standard Apple TV transport expectations for play, pause, scrub, and skip.

## Guardrails

- Do not bury focusable controls against screen edges.
- Do not keep a permanently dense overlay over the media.
- Do not make focus travel through long chains of low-value controls before transport actions.
